### 1. Setting Your Availability

**Location:** On the top navigation bar, you'll see **"Availability"**

**Step-by-Step:**

1. **Navigate to Availability**
   - Click on **"Availability"** in the top navigation bar
   - Or go directly to: `/therapist/dashboard/availability`

2. **Configure Your Schedule**
   - Select your **available days** (Monday through Sunday)
   - Set your **working hours** for each day
   - Choose your **time zone** (important for accurate scheduling)
   - Click **"Save Availability"** when done

3. **Set Recurring Availability** (Optional)
   - Use the **"Generate Weekly Template"** feature to quickly set up recurring schedules
   - This allows you to copy your availability from one week to another

4. **Override Specific Dates** (Optional)
   - If you need to block specific dates or change hours for a particular day
   - Use the **"Override Availability"** feature to set one-time exceptions

### 2. Accessing Video Sessions

**Location:** Video sessions are accessible from multiple places in your dashboard

**Step-by-Step:**

#### **Option A: From Dashboard Overview**
1. Go to your **Dashboard** (`/therapist/dashboard`)
2. Look for the **"Upcoming Sessions"** section
3. Find the session you want to join
4. Click **"Join Session"** or **"Start Video Call"** button
5. You'll be redirected to the video call room

#### **Option B: From Sessions Page**
1. Navigate to **"Client Sessions"** in the sidebar
2. Find your session in the list (filter by status: "Upcoming", "In Progress", etc.)
3. Click on the session card
4. Click **"Join Video Call"** button

#### **Option C: Direct Video Call Page**
1. Go to **"Video Call"** in the sidebar (`/therapist/dashboard/video-call`)
2. Select the session you want to join from the list
3. Click **"Join Session"**
